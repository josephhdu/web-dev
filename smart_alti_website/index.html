Smart Altimeter

It's time for a cheap ditter to enter the market. For all too long, skydiving equipment was priced at incredibly high costs. With this altimeter using parts sourced from the car industry to provide reliable and cheap alternatives, we have slashed the cost of competitors.

Our altimeter can detect when you go up for your joyride and intelligently put itself to sleep in storage. With the modern USB-C connection, you no longer have to worry about using an old cable. After your jump, once connected to wifi, it will upload your data to your personal account, allowing you to view your logbook and a recap of your last jump.
